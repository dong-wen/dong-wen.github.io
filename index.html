ABOUT ME

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow in the University of Technology Sydney and also an adjunct associate lecturer in the University of New South Wales. I received my Ph.D. degree in 2019 from the University of Technology Sydney, Australia. Prior to that, I received my Bachelor degree from the Nankai University, China in 2013.
